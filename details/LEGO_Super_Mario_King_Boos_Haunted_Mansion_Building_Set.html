LEGO Super Mario King Boo's Haunted Mansion Building Set

Step into the spooky world of Super Mario with this exciting LEGO building set. Kids will love exploring the haunted mansion, searching for the key to the treasure chest, and battling ghostly enemies. This set is packed with detailed pieces and features that bring the spooky adventure to life.

Inside, you'll find 4 collectible figures: King Boo, a Yellow Baby Yoshi, a Dry Bones, and a Boo. These figures are ideal for imaginative storytelling and role play, encouraging kids to create their own fun-filled mysteries. The set includes 932 high-quality LEGO bricks, designed for precise and creative construction.

Even better, the interactive digital play feature allows children to guide Mario, Luigi, or Peach (figures not included) through the adventure on their devices. The companion LEGO Super Mario app adds interactive elements and building tips, helping kids build with confidence and play with excitement.

With its elevating sofa, key-reveal bookcase, and special blocks, this set offers an immersive building and play experience. Whether playing alone or with friends, children will enjoy endless storytelling and digital challenges that keep the fun going.

  • Elevating sofa and key-reveal bookcase
  • Expandable design for added play possibilities
