LEGO ONE PIECE Battle at Arlong Park Building Set (75638)

This LEGO ONE PIECE building set is a fantastic way for young fans to dive into the world of the anime. Designed for kids aged 9+, it combines creativity and adventure with an array of interactive features that bring the story to life.

Immersive Storytelling with Action

With its detailed pagoda structure and dramatic elements like the exploding pagoda, this set ensures hours of excitement. Kids can build and play the epic Fishman showdown scene, reliving the moments from their favorite episodes in a fun, hands-on way.

Characters for Every Adventure

Five high-quality minifigures, including Luffy, Nami, Usopp, Arlong, and Chu, come with unique accessories. From wanted posters to a ketchup bottle, every detail adds to the authenticity of the experience, making it ideal for imaginative play and storytelling.

Perfect for Creative Kids

Whether it's for a birthday or a special occasion, this LEGO set makes a great gift for boys and girls who love anime and building toys. The easy-to-follow building instructions and LEGO Builder app support help parents and kids work together, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable assembly process.

- Engaging and fun for young anime enthusiasts
- Interactive elements like the exploding pagoda and shooting gallery enhance play
- Highly detailed minifigures for immersive storytelling
- Build and play with a rich, real-world environment
- Perfectly suited for ages 9+ to develop creativity and motor skills
